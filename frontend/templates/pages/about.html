We’re dog-lovers, cat-fanatics, bird-fans and more

We understand that losing a pet is an incredibly heart-breaking thing to go through so we wanted to create a space where owners can eternally memorialise their pet by hosting personalised pages that can be visited, shared and loved.

Our pets’ lives are about the memories, the photos, the stories, the favourite toys, the funny adventures, the quirky habits and, most importantly, the lasting impact they leave on us.

Compile all these memories in one place with Pets Forever. Tails never forgotten…
